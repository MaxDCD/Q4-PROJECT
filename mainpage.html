A little bit more about this site...

Why is this the theme? Well, the theme of my website is the man, the myth, and the incredible Aryton Senna, a legendary Brazilian Formula 1 driver with 3 WDCs (World Drivers' Championship) who passed away 30 years ago (May 1, 1994) during the San Marino Grand Prix. He was known to dominate the Monaco track, where he won 6 races, making him the "King of Monaco".

As the Monaco Grand Prix is 2 days near (May 26, 2024), F1 launched an event dedicated to Senna's 30-year death anniversary. I took inspiration from Sebastian Vettel's Imola track marathon for Senna and him driving Aryton's infamous Mclaren 1993 car around the Imola track, and Mclaren's release of their new Senna livery & merchandise for the upcoming Monaco GP. And so, I decided to dedicate this website to not only my project and grade, but to the legend Aryton Senna as a way to tribute him as well and to make sure his legacy will be known and remembered.
