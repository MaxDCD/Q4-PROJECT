A little bit more about this site...

Why is this the theme? Well, the theme of my website is Aryton Senna, a legendary Brazilian Formula 1 driver with 3 WDCs (World Drivers' Championship) who passed away 30 years ago (May 1, 1994) during the San Marino Grand Prix. He was known to dominate the Monaco track, where he won 6 races, making him the "King of Monaco". As the Monaco Grand Prix is 2 days near (May 26, 2024), I took inspiration from Seb Vettel's track marathon for Senna at Imola, Vettel driving Aryton's infamous Mclaren 1993 car at Imola as well, and Mclaren's release of their new Senna tribute livery for the upcoming Monaco GP. And so, I decided to dedicate this website to not only my project and grade, but to the legend Aryton Senna as a way to tribute him as well and to make his legacy unforgettable in my book.
